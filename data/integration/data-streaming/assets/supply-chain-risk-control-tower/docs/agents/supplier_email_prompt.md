# Supplier Email Prompt

You are helping a procurement manager write a supplier escalation email.

Use the risk event and recommendation to draft a concise but firm email asking the supplier to confirm recovery actions.

## Input

```json
{{risk_event}}
```

```json
{{recommendation_event}}
```

## Requirements

- Keep the tone professional and collaborative.
- Ask for confirmed ETA, recovery plan, partial shipment option, and escalation contact.
- Mention business impact without sounding accusatory.
- Keep it under 180 words.

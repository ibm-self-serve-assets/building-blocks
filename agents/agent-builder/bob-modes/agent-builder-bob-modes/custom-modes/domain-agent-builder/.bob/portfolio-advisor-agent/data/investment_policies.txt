# Investment Policies and Guidelines

## Rebalancing Policy

Portfolio rebalancing is triggered when any asset class drifts more than 5% from its target allocation. Rebalancing reviews are conducted quarterly for Bronze and Silver tiers, monthly for Gold tier, and continuously (real-time monitoring) for Diamond tier.

### Rebalancing Thresholds by Tier

| Tier | Drift Threshold | Review Frequency | Execution Window |
|------|----------------|------------------|------------------|
| Bronze | 7% | Quarterly | 5 business days |
| Silver | 5% | Quarterly | 3 business days |
| Gold | 5% | Monthly | 2 business days |
| Diamond | 3% | Continuous | Same day |

### Tax-Loss Harvesting

Tax-loss harvesting is available for Gold and Diamond tier accounts only. Minimum unrealized loss threshold for harvesting is $5,000. Wash sale rules require a 31-day waiting period before repurchasing substantially identical securities.

## Prohibited Investments

The following investments are prohibited across all tiers:
- Cryptocurrency and digital assets (under review for 2027)
- Penny stocks (securities trading below $1.00)
- Leveraged ETFs with more than 3x leverage
- Unregistered securities and private placements under $100,000 (except Diamond tier)
- Options strategies involving naked short positions (except Gold and Diamond with signed risk disclosure)

## Emergency Withdrawal Policy

Account holders may request emergency withdrawals outside of normal processing windows. Emergency withdrawals are processed within 24 hours but incur a 0.5% expedited processing fee for Bronze and Silver tiers. Gold and Diamond tiers receive emergency withdrawals at no additional charge.

## Account Upgrade Policy

Account holders are eligible for tier upgrade when their portfolio value has been consistently above the next tier's minimum for 3 consecutive months. Upon upgrade, the new tier's fee schedule takes effect on the first day of the following month. Tier benefits are activated immediately upon upgrade confirmation.

## Annual Review Requirements

All accounts receive a mandatory annual portfolio review:
- Bronze: Written summary report mailed or emailed
- Silver: 30-minute phone or video call with an advisor
- Gold: 60-minute in-depth review with a senior advisor, including tax planning
- Diamond: Full-day wealth planning session with a dedicated advisory team, covering estate planning, tax optimization, and philanthropic strategy
